Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FlatOrg ("the app") is a household task management app for co-living flats. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Display name — shown to other members of your flat.
  • User ID — an internal identifier generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • App usage data — task assignments, shopping list entries, and issue reports you create within the app, stored in Firestore and visible only to members of your flat.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality: account management, flat membership, task scheduling, and in-app notifications. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud), governed by Google's privacy and security practices.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. An admin can also remove members from within the app.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or deletion requests, contact: the flat admin.
